Diddles is the marching percussion practice tool that you have been waiting for. This app lets you feel what it is like to practice with a drumline.

Diddles is now available on iTunes.

Diddles gives you the ability to:

  • Play along in a sectional or a full drumline
  • View the music as you listen to the exercise
  • Set the number of times that you want to play an exercise
  • Choose the beats per minute setting to the level you want to play
  • Auto increment beats per minute
  • Fine control over the volume of each instrument

For the best experience please use headphones with this app.
