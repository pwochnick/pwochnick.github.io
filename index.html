Personal Profile

Paul Wochnick joined KPBS in 2007 and is responsible for all areas of the station’s information technology needs and infrastructure, from computing networks to server maintenance. He possesses extensive experience in system administration, network management, user support, and security. Paul has earned a number professional certificates and accreditations. He is a Microsoft Certified Systems Administrator (MCSA ) and an Apple Certified Support Professional (ACSP). Prior to joining KPBS, Paul worked at Twin Cities Public Television and the Minneapolis College of Art and Design. He earned his bachelor’s degree in information technology from Metropolitan State University.
